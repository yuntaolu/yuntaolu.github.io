About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Bei Yu and co-supervised by Prof. Keren Zhu since 2023 Fall. Previously, I received my M.S. at the University of Texas at Austin (UT-Austin) and the University of Science and Technology of China(USTC), and B.Eng. at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China(UESTC). My research interests are mainly about Artificial Intelligence and Functional Verification in EDA.
